About Mayur Colour

Mayur Colour & Chemicals, established in 1992, is a trusted manufacturer and exporter of Acid, Direct, Solvent, and Basic Dyes along with Organic Pigments, serving global markets from its Ankleshwar G.I.D.C. manufacturing unit with a strong focus on quality.

Our Story

Founded in 1992, Mayur Colour & Chemicals started with a strong foundation of industry knowledge and technical expertise in dyes and pigments.Since 1993, we have been manufacturing and exporting a wide range of dyes and pigments to global markets.Our growth is driven by consistent quality, customer trust, and decades of hands-on experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to manufacture high-quality dyes and pigments that meet customer specifications through strict quality control and advanced production practices. We aim to build long-term relationships by delivering consistent quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction worldwide.
